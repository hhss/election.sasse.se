It’s time to vote!

From Tuesday morning until Thursday the 6th at 17:00 you’ll be able to vote, this is how it will work: Those who have paid the SASSE-fee will get an email with a link to the voting site. Instructions on how to vote will be on the site, just follow them and make the important decisions. The voting is definitive; you cannot change your choices afterwards.

The results will be presented during the Election Pub in the Rotunda at 8 p.m. Thursday the 6th of December.

See you there!
